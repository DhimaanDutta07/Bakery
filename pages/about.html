Our Story

From a tiny home kitchen to Mumbai's most loved bakery

Founded 2018

A Dream Baked in Mumbai

Sweet Haven began in 2018 when Kavya Nair returned from Paris after training at Le Cordon Bleu. Armed with French technique and a deep love for Indian flavours, she set up a tiny kitchen in Bandra and started taking orders via WhatsApp.

Word spread fast. Her Gulab Jamun croissant — a fusion pastry that combined French lamination with rosewater-cardamom flavours — went viral on Instagram. Within six months, she had a waitlist of 200 customers.

Today, Sweet Haven is a 12-person team baking over 50 items daily, serving thousands of Mumbaikars who believe life is better with great bread and even better cake.
